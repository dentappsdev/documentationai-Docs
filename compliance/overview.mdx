## What is the Compliance Module?

The Compliance module in ChairPulse provides automated compliance tracking for dental practices. It generates a personalized compliance plan based on your office location, practice type, and the equipment you have on file. Once activated, the system creates recurring tasks on a timeline so your team can stay on top of regulatory requirements without manually tracking deadlines.

Compliance requirements vary by state, and the engine maps federal CDC guidelines and state dental board rules to the specific equipment in your practice. The result is a tailored set of tasks -- each with a due date, a frequency, and a specific completion method (called a "primitive").

<Callout kind="alert">
  The Compliance Wizard and full compliance tracking features require a **Professional tier** subscription. If you are on a lower tier, you will need to upgrade before you can generate a compliance plan.
</Callout>

## How Compliance Works

At a high level, the compliance workflow follows three stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up with the wizard">
    The [setup wizard](/compliance/setup-wizard) walks you through confirming your practice profile, reviewing your equipment, and answering a few practice-specific questions. The system then generates a compliance plan tailored to your office.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm requirements">
    After the wizard generates tasks, you enter [requirement review](/compliance/requirement-review) mode. Here you confirm which requirements apply to your practice and dismiss any that do not.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete tasks on the dashboard">
    Once your plan is active, the [compliance dashboard](/compliance/compliance-dashboard) shows your tasks organized by priority. Complete tasks as they come due to maintain compliance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The 8 Compliance Categories

Every compliance requirement falls into one of eight categories. Each category groups related equipment and regulatory obligations together.

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| **Radiation Equipment** | X-ray machines, CBCT, panoramic units |
| **Sterilization Equipment** | Autoclaves, sterilizers, instrument processing |
| **Sedation Systems** | Nitrous oxide, anesthesia equipment |
| **Water Systems** | Waterlines, filtration, treatment |
| **Air & Vacuum Systems** | Compressors, vacuums, HVE |
| **Safety Equipment** | Emergency equipment, protective gear |
| **Waste Management** | Amalgam separators, sharps, biohazard |
| **Other** | Miscellaneous equipment requirements |

## Prerequisites

Before you can use the Compliance module, your office profile must have:

- A **state** set (determines which state-specific regulations apply)
- A **practice type** set (e.g., General Dentistry, Pediatric, Orthodontics)

If either is missing, the Compliance page will prompt you to complete your practice profile in **Office Settings** before proceeding.

## Task Completion Primitives

Each compliance task uses one of six completion methods, called "primitives." These determine how you record that a task has been done:

- **Heartbeat** -- Simple confirmation that the task was completed
- **Inspector** -- Pass/fail inspection with optional lot number
- **Meter** -- Numeric reading compared against a threshold
- **Clock** -- Expiration date tracking with advance notice warnings
- **Certificate** -- Upload a certificate with service details
- **Auto** -- Automatically managed by connected equipment

Learn more about each primitive in the dedicated pages below.
