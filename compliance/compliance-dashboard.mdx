## Overview

The Compliance Dashboard is the central hub for managing your practice's compliance. It provides an at-a-glance view of your compliance posture with risk summary cards, a prioritized task timeline, and quick access to task completion.

The dashboard appears automatically after you complete the [setup wizard](/compliance/setup-wizard) and [requirement review](/compliance/requirement-review) process.

## Page Header

The dashboard header shows:

- **Title**: "Compliance"
- **Tracking summary**: The number of requirements tracked out of the total applicable to your state, along with your office state and practice type
- **Action buttons**:
  - **Requirements** -- View the full requirements list
  - **History** -- Navigate to the [compliance history](/compliance/compliance-history) page
  - **Ask AI** -- Open the AI assistant panel for compliance questions
  - **Add Task** -- Manually add a custom compliance task
  - **More menu** (three-dot icon):
    - **Inspector View** -- Open the [inspector-friendly view](/compliance/inspector-view)
    - **Reconfigure...** -- Reset and restart the compliance setup (destructive action)

## Risk Summary Cards

The risk summary section displays three cards in a row when there are overdue tasks:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **At Risk** | Number of overdue task instances, broken down by compliance category (e.g., "3 Sterilization, 1 Radiation"). Pulsing red dot indicator. |
| **On Track** | Number of requirements that are current and not overdue. |
| **Auto-Managed** | Number of tasks automatically tracked by connected equipment. |

When there are no overdue tasks, the three cards are replaced with a single compressed success banner showing the total on-track count and how many are auto-managed.

## Prioritized Task Timeline

The main body of the dashboard is the task timeline, which organizes tasks into priority sections:

### Overdue

Tasks that have passed their due date without being completed. These appear at the top with the highest visual priority.

### Due Today

Tasks due on the current date.

### This Week

Upcoming tasks due within the current week.

### Next Week

Tasks due in the following week.

### Later

Tasks due further in the future. This section initially shows a collapsed preview (5 items) with a button to expand and view all.

### Awaiting Results

Tasks that have been submitted (e.g., mail-in spore test sent) but are waiting for results to come back.

Each task row in the timeline displays:

- Task title and category badge
- Due date
- Assigned staff member (with an assignment dropdown to change)
- A completion button to open the [task completion modal](/compliance/completing-tasks)

For per-equipment tasks (e.g., the same requirement applied to multiple autoclaves), rows are grouped together. You can complete all units at once using the group completion button.

## First-Visit Banner

When you first access the dashboard after setup, a blue info banner appears:

> "Your compliance plan is ready! You have X tasks across Y categories. Click any task to assign staff, adjust schedules, and start tracking."

This banner can be dismissed by clicking the close button.

## Completing Tasks from the Dashboard

Click the completion button on any task row to open the [Task Completion Modal](/compliance/completing-tasks). The modal displays the appropriate completion form based on the task's primitive type (heartbeat, inspector, meter, clock, certificate, or auto).

## Reconfiguring Compliance

If you need to start over (e.g., your practice moved to a new state or changed practice type), use the **Reconfigure...** option from the more menu. This is a destructive action that:

- Permanently deletes all compliance tasks
- Removes all completion history and uploaded evidence
- Removes your compliance setup configuration
- Requires you to go through the full setup wizard again

To confirm, you must type "RESET" and your full name as acknowledgment.

<Callout kind="danger">
  Reconfiguring compliance is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider editing individual tasks directly from the dashboard instead, unless your practice fundamentals have changed (new state, different practice type).
</Callout>
