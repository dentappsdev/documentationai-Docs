## Your SOPs and training in one place

The **Knowledge & Training** page is your central hub for managing standard operating procedures (SOPs) and team training. It brings together everything your practice needs to document workflows, maintain consistency, and onboard new team members.

Navigate to **Knowledge** from the main sidebar to open this page.

{/* Screenshot needed: Knowledge & Training page showing the two tabs (Training Paths and SOP Library) with the page header */}

## Page layout

The page is organized with a header and two tabs:

- **Page header** with the title "Knowledge & Training", the subtitle "Your SOPs and training paths in one place", and action buttons that change based on the active tab.
- **Training Paths tab** -- Browse and manage structured learning sequences for your team (shown by default).
- **SOP Library tab** -- Browse, search, and organize your standard operating procedures by category.

Switch between tabs by selecting **Training Paths** or **SOP Library** in the tab bar below the header.

## Header actions

The action buttons in the page header change depending on which tab is active:

### SOP Library tab actions

- **Create New SOP** -- Opens the [SOP creation wizard](/knowledge/creating-sops) where you can choose how to create your procedure.
- **Ask SOP Assistant** -- Opens the [SOP Assistant](/knowledge/sop-assistant) AI chat to ask questions about your existing SOPs.

### Training Paths tab actions (Professional tier)

- **Create Path** -- Opens the dialog to [create a new training path](/knowledge/creating-training-paths).
- **Browse Templates** -- Opens the template browser with pre-built training path templates (visible when you already have at least one path).
- **Create Category** -- Creates a new category to organize your training paths.

<Callout kind="alert">
Training path creation requires the **Professional** tier. Essential tier users see an upgrade prompt instead of the Create Path button.
</Callout>

## Tier limits

The Knowledge module has usage limits that vary by subscription tier:

| Feature | Essential | Professional |
|---|---|---|
| AI SOP Generation | 25/month | Unlimited |
| SOP Chat Messages | 1,000/month | 3,000/month |
| Comprehensive SOPs (search-verified) | Not available | Available |
| Training Paths | View only | Create and manage |
