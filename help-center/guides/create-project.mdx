---

## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    On your dashboard, click the "Projects" tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Project">
    Press the "+ New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out details">
    Enter the project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Invite Team">
    Click "Create" to finish. Invite teammates to collaborate.
  </Step>
</Steps>
