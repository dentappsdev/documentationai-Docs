---
title: Cannot Invite a User
description: Fix user invitation issues in ChairPulse.
---

## Symptoms

- "Invitation failed" error
- Team member does not receive invite email

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Ensure the email is entered correctly and user has not already joined.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask user to check spam">
    Sometimes invitations land in the spam folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend invite">
    Go to the workspace > Members > Resend invitation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
