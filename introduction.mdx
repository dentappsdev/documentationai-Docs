## Overview

ChairPulse is the intelligent equipment operations platform built specifically for dental practices. You manage proactive maintenance, AI-powered diagnostics, and multi-jurisdiction compliance all in one unified dashboard. Set up your office operations to run efficiently like a large DSO, with deployment in just 1-3 days.

The platform auto-populates maintenance tasks for your equipment, generates standard operating procedures (SOPs), and ensures regulatory compliance based on your location. Access it at `https://app.chairpulse.com`.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with ChairPulse in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://app.chairpulse.com`. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Equipment" icon="database">
    Select your dental chairs, autoclaves, and other devices from the catalog. ChairPulse auto-populates tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Location" icon="map-pin">
    Choose your jurisdiction (e.g., California) to load compliance rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Tasks" icon="checklist">
