## Managing service providers

Service providers are the technicians, repair companies, and service vendors that your practice works with for equipment maintenance and repairs. Keeping an up-to-date list of service providers in ChairPulse makes it easy to reference their contact information when logging tech visits and resolving issues.

Access the service providers list from the **Service Technicians** tab in [Office Settings](/settings/office-settings).

## The service providers table

The **Service Providers** card displays all providers for your current office in a table with the following columns:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | The technician's name, with a "Preferred" badge (gold star) if marked as preferred |
| **Company** | The service company name |
| **Specialty** | The provider's area of expertise (e.g., "Dental Chairs", "X-Ray") |
| **Contact** | Quick-action icons for calling (phone), emailing (mail), or visiting their website (globe) |
| **Actions** | Three-dot menu with Edit and Remove options |

Providers marked as **Preferred** are sorted to the top of the list.

## Adding a service provider

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Provider">
    Click the **Add Provider** button in the top right of the Service Providers card. This opens the Add Service Provider dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the technician's name">
    The **Technician Name** field is required. Enter the individual technician's name (e.g., "John Smith").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the company name">
    Optionally enter the **Company Name** (e.g., "ABC Dental Services").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add contact information">
    Fill in any of the optional contact fields:
    - **Phone** -- The technician's phone number
    - **Email** -- Their email address
    - **Website** -- Their company website URL
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a specialty">
    Optionally enter their **Specialty** (e.g., "Dental Chairs", "X-Ray", "Autoclaves").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes">
    Use the **Notes** field for any additional information about this provider (e.g., "Available weekends", "Prefers email contact").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as preferred (optional)">
    Toggle the **Preferred Provider** switch to mark this provider as a go-to contact. Preferred providers appear at the top of the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Provider">
    Click **Add Provider** to save. The provider immediately appears in the table.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a service provider

Click the three-dot menu on any provider row and select **Edit**. The same dialog opens with all fields pre-filled. Make your changes and click **Save Changes**.

## Removing a service provider

Click the three-dot menu on any provider row and select **Remove**. The provider is deleted immediately.

<Callout kind="info">
Removing a service provider does not affect existing tech visit records or invoice data that reference that provider. Historical data is preserved.
</Callout>

## Auto-suggested providers from invoices

When you upload an invoice that identifies a new vendor not yet in your service providers list, ChairPulse can automatically suggest adding them. In the [Invoice Details](/invoices/uploading-invoices) modal, you will see a prompt:

> "Save this vendor as a service provider? Add [Vendor Name] to your contacts for easy access next time."

Click **Add to Service Providers** to create the provider entry automatically with the name, phone, and email extracted from the invoice.

## How service providers connect to issues

Service providers are referenced in several places throughout ChairPulse:

- **Tech visit records** -- When logging a technician visit on an issue, you can select from your service providers list.
- **Invoice reconciliation** -- ChairPulse compares the vendor name on invoices against your service providers and tech visit records.
- **Issue resolution** -- Knowing which providers specialize in which equipment helps you assign the right technician for the job.
