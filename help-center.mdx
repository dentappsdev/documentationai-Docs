## Help Center vs documentation

Use these guidelines to decide where to look first.

- **Help Center**  
  Use when you are blocked, seeing an error, or need a short, goal-focused answer.

- **Product documentation**  
  Use when you want to understand concepts, explore features, or learn how ChairPulse fits into your workflow. Start from the [Introduction](/introduction) or [Getting Started](/getting-started).

## Still stuck?

Follow these steps to get unstuck as quickly as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search the Help Center" icon="search">

Type a few words that describe your issue into search, such as `invite teammate`, `create project`, or the exact error message. Open any result that closely matches what you are seeing.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse troubleshooting articles" icon="alert-triangle">

If search does not help, open the **Troubleshooting** section and scan for your symptom. Articles are named by the visible problem, such as **Cannot Invite User**.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact support with details" icon="mail">

If you still cannot resolve the issue, use **Email Support** above. Include what you tried, any error messages, and a screenshot when possible so the team can help faster.

  </Step>
</Steps>
