## What is ChairPulse?

ChairPulse is a purpose-built platform for dental practices that centralizes everything related to your equipment operations. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, paper logs, and memory-based maintenance schedules, ChairPulse gives your team a single place to manage equipment, stay compliant, and troubleshoot problems faster.

{/* Screenshot needed: Dashboard overview showing QuickStatsRow with maintenance due, active issues, awaiting review, and upcoming deadlines */}

When you sign in, the **Dashboard** gives you an at-a-glance view of what needs attention: maintenance tasks that are due or overdue, active equipment issues, invoices awaiting review, and upcoming compliance deadlines. Alert bars for **Issues** and **Compliance** highlight items that need immediate action.

<Callout kind="info" title="Vitals for every operatory">

ChairPulse is designed for dental office managers, practice owners, and clinical staff. No technical background is needed. The platform auto-generates maintenance schedules and compliance tasks based on your equipment and location.

</Callout>

## What you see in the sidebar

The main navigation in ChairPulse is organized into these sections:

**Main**
- **Dashboard** -- Your command center with quick stats, alert bars, and maintenance tasks
- **Equipment** -- Full equipment inventory with detail pages for each item
- **Issues** -- Log and track equipment problems with AI diagnostics
- **Maintenance** -- Scheduled maintenance tasks with completion tracking
- **Checklists** -- Operational checklists for daily and periodic routines
- **Compliance** -- Regulatory compliance tasks organized by category
- **Knowledge** -- SOPs, the SOP Assistant, and learning paths
- **Invoice Analytics** -- Track and reconcile equipment service invoices
- **Reports** -- Practice-wide reporting and analytics

**Settings** (collapsible)
- **Rooms & Locations** -- Define operatories, sterilization rooms, labs, and other spaces
- **Office Settings** -- Practice-level configuration
- **Organization** -- Multi-office and team management (visible to admin and DSO admin roles)
