## Recent Updates

<Callout kind="info">
Stay current with ChairPulse enhancements. Subscribe to our release notifications to receive emails about major updates and breaking changes.

ChairPulse releases updates regularly to improve maintenance tracking, AI diagnostics, and compliance features for dental equipment. Review the changelog below to see what's new.

<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>
## New Features

- Added AI-powered photo analysis for equipment diagnostics—upload images of dental chairs or autoclaves for instant issue detection.
- Introduced multi-jurisdiction compliance presets for California, Texas, and New York, auto-linking spore testing and waterline requirements to your equipment inventory.

## Improvements

- Enhanced weekly spore testing SOP generation with customizable materials lists and step-by-step logging.
- Optimized dashboard performance for practices with 50+ equipment items, reducing load times by 40%.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed pressure error logging for Midmark M11 autoclaves during AI diagnosis workflows.
- Resolved scheduling conflicts in proactive maintenance tasks for A-Dec 500 dental chairs.
</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-20" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "bugfix"]}>
## New Features

- Launched service visit tracking with status updates (Logged, Diagnosing, Scheduled, Visited, Resolved) and cost logging.
- Integrated equipment selection for auto-populated tasks, including handpiece coupler lubrication reminders.

## Improvements

- Improved AI diagnostic confidence scoring, now displaying with recommended steps and "Still need help?" escalation.

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected compliance requirement visibility when switching jurisdictions.
- Fixed log sheet export issues for generated SOPs like weekly spore testing protocols.
</Update>

<Update label="2024-08-10" description="v1.1.0" tags={["improvement", "bugfix"]}>
## Improvements

- Streamlined setup process—now operational in 1-3 days with automated equipment import.
- Added dashboard widgets for compliance status (You're Compliant) and active requirements.

## Bug Fixes

- Patched gasket replacement cost tracking in resolved issues.
- Resolved incubation time display errors in sterilization cycle SOPs.
</Update>
